A Fantastic Tomato Soup Recipe

A Recipe for Making Some Healthy and Filling Tomato Soup

This is the perfect recipe for all you health-conscious people out there!

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp of Tomato Paste
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • A Handful of Fresh Basil
  • 1.5L of Water

Steps

  1. Add the 1.5L of water into a pot and start to get it boiling.
  2. While the water starts to boil, dice the onion.
  3. Fry the diced onions in a pan until translucent.
  4. Add the tomato paste and fry for 3 more minutes.
  5. Your water should be boiling by now. Empty the can of chopped tomatoes into the pot of water.
  6. Add your fried onion/tomato paste mixture into the pot.
  7. Let your pot simmer over a gentle heat for 20 to 30 minutes.
  8. Once done, use a hand blender to get your soup to the desired consistency.
  9. Garnish the soup with some fresh basil.
  10. Enjoy!
