Tomato Soup
  • Ingredients:
  • 1 Can O' Chopped Maters
  • 1/2 A Cup O' Chopped Onion Pieces
  • 1 Tablespoon o' Olive Oil
  • Salt 'N' Pepper
  • 1 Cup o'
    H2O
Is it food or is it drink?
I'll need a moment just to think.
It tastes fantastic either way.
This rouge-y soup will make your day.
  • 1. Place your olive oil into a decent-ish sized pot and heat it up!
  • 2. Find it within yourself to cook those innocent onions (until golden).
  • 4. Empty your can of maters and good ol'
    H2O
    into the pot.
  • 5. Let your onion/mater/water ('OniMatWat') concoction cook for about 15 mins over a medium heat.
  • 6. Turn off the heat and let things chill out for a bit.
  • 7. Once things have cooled down, put your 'OniMatWat' into a blender.
  • 8. Blend (lid on please!) until desired consistency has been established.
  • 9. Put the soup into a bowl (duh!) and add some salt 'n' pepper with a flourish.
You made some soup; Achieved your goal.
