A Delicious Banana Smoothie Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 1 Medium Sized Banana;
  • Two tbsp Smooth Peanut Butter;
  • Two tbsp Vanilla Yoghurt;
  • 1/2 a glass of Milk. (Milk Substitutes are ok too!)
A Quick and Easy Recipe for Making an Indulgent Banana Smoothie
This recipe will have you saying "Wow!" with just how easily you can whip up a scrumptious yellow-fruited beverage...
    Steps
  1. Cut the banana into pieces and place into a blender.
  2. Add the two tbsp of peanut butter and vanilla yoghurt into the blender.
  3. Pour the milk (or milk substitute) into the blender.
  4. Blend all of your ingredients together. (Don't forget to put the lid on - unless you want to make a big mess!)
  5. Enjoy!
