Banana and Peanut Butter Smoothie
    Ingredients:
  • 1 'Nana
  • 2 Tablespoons o' Smooth Peanut Butter
  • 2 Tablespoons o' Vanilla yogurt
  • 1/2 A Glass o' Cow Juice (Or substitute o' choice!)
A fruit from above, a 'gume from the earth.
They make a fine drink when they're mixed-up with mirth.
If you take your first sip in the morning light,
You'll remember the taste when your dreams take flight.
  1. 1. Spoon up your peanut butter and your vanilla yogurt and jettison them into your blender.
  2. 2. Break up your 'nana into chunks.
  3. 3. Slam dunk your chunked up 'nana pieces into the aforementioned blender.
  4. 4. Pour your cow juice (or substitute o' choice) into...guess where!
  5. 5. Unless you want to spend the next eternity cleaning your kitchen, put the lid on your blender!
  6. 6. Smash (gently) that blend button.
