A Recipe to Make an American Classic: S'Mores!

An Indoor Twist on a Campfire Classic.

This recipe will make you feel like you're truly adventuring in the great American outdoors.

Ingredients

  • 4 Graham Crackers
  • 4 Marshmallows
  • 1 Large Bar of Hershey's Chocolate

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450ºF/232ºC.
  2. Line your baking tray in some parchment paper.
  3. Break each Graham Cracker in half and arrange them on your baking tray.
  4. Place one marshmallow on four of the Graham Cracker halves.
  5. Put the baking tray in the oven and bake for about 5 minutes (until the marshmallows are golden brown).
  6. While the baking tray is still in the oven, break your chocolate bar into pieces.
  7. Remove the baking tray from the oven and place a piece of chocolate on top of the marshmallow-covered Graham Cracker.
  8. Complete your S'More by placing one of the empty Graham Cracker halves over a marshmallow/chocolate covered half.
  9. Enjoy!
